# Romo AI Domain Model

## Purpose

This document defines the language of Romo AI.

Romo AI is not built around AI providers.
Romo AI is built around business reality.

## Core Rule

The Business is the center of the system.

AI is not the source of truth.
The system is the source of truth.

## Core Models

- Business
- Metric
- Agent
- Task
- Insight
- Recommendation
- Integration
- Customer
- Supplier

## Architecture Principle

Do not create one giant Business model.

Use small models with one responsibility.

## Why This Matters

If the domain model is clean, the dashboard, agents, memory, reports, and AI layer will be easier to build and maintain.
